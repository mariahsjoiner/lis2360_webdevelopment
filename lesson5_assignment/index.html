Inequality in Information Technology

Overview | Testimonials | Author

Information Technology (IT) is one of the fastest growing fields for careers. While IT has mostly been a male dominated profession, in the last 20 years, more woman have started to choose IT as their careers. For many women in the tech world, being the only woman in a room full of men is a common occurance. Statistics showing women holding a technical role are low, and according to a 2015 report by CNET, companies such as Google, Facebook, Microsoft, and Twitter have less then 20 percent women in technical roles. Female IT students often look at these statistics and see a successful future in tech as scarce. But what is it about IT that has has women second guessing choosing IT related careers?

Meet The Author

Shelby Joiner is a 24 year old Information, Communication and Technology student at Florida State University. This site was created to spread awareness and challenge gender inequalities in the technology field. She looks forward to graduating and continuing a long career in IT.



         

This is a color palette selected to highlight the pink accents in the cover photo. I chose this palette to emphasize feminity throughout the website without causing distractions.
